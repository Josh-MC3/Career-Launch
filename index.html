Most career sites repeat these to scare you into buying something. Here's what the research actually shows.

"6 seconds to read your resume?" Mostly a myth

It comes from a 30-person study with thin methodology. Real review time runs closer to 30 seconds — and longer if you pass the first glance.

See the research → "75% auto-rejected by robots?" Debunked

The number traces to a 2012 sales pitch from a company that went out of business. 92% of recruiters say their systems don't auto-reject at all.

See the research → "Beat the ATS and you're in." Not how it works
